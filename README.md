# #76 Fortune Cookie

A digital "toy" that provides irresponsible advice, bizarre facts, and meaningless predictions. It captures the essence of a physical fortune cookie: a brief, crunchy moment of curiosity followed by a paper-thin revelation.

## Core Philosophy
- **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Tap the cookie. That's it.
- **Pure Whimsy**: Unlike other tools in the Fridge Combo series, this tool offers zero utility. Its only purpose is to provide a "smile" or a "shrug."
- **Enforced Reflection**: A 3-second "baking time" ensures you spend a moment with your current fortune before chasing the next one.

## Features
- **Dynamic Cracking Animation**: The cookie physically splits apart upon tapping, creating a tactile "crack" experience.
- **Shredder Reveal**: Messages emerge from below like a strip of paper being fed through a shredder.
- **Auto-Suction Reset**: After 3 seconds, the message is sucked upward into the "void," and a fresh cookie is baked in its place.
- **Dynamic Font Resizing**: Handles anything from short one-liners to longer, cynical observations without breaking the layout.
- **Last Fortune Log**: Keeps a faint record of your previous result so you can double-check the absurdity you just read.

## Design System
- **Background**: #fff
- **Main Color**: #333
- **Border Radius**: 15px (Modals and Buttons)
- **Animation**: Smooth 0.4s transitions for a high-quality "toy" feel.

## Privacy & Performance
- **No Login / No Tracking**: Your "destiny" is local to your browser.
- **No Ads**: A clean, distraction-free environment for your daily dose of nonsense.
- **Browser Only**: Lightweight HTML/CSS/JS with no external dependencies.
- **Mobile Optimized**: Smooth animations even on low-spec devices and standard mobile browsers.
